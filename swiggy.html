DoC Band
Methodology: DoC = Total Inventory ÷ Daily Run Rate (DRR). Velocity computed from trailing-30-day city-level sales. Warehouse-level DoC uses inventory-share allocation: a warehouse holding X% of the city's inventory is assumed to serve X% of the city's velocity. By this allocation, all warehouses in a city share the same DoC — what differs is concentration (where the inventory sits) and absolute units.
